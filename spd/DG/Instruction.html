During this block, you will be asked to make decisions in 5 different situations. You will be randomly paired with another anonymous participant. Your decisions in one decision situation have no influence on other decision situations.

In each decision situation, one of you and your paired participant is the DICTATOR who can decide the final payoffs of both of you, another is the RECEIVER who can only receive the payoffs unconditionally.

At the end of the experiment, the ROLE of you and your randomly paired participant will be RANDOMLY ASSIGNED.

Once a decision situation ends, you can move to another new decision situation.
