During this block, you will be asked to make decisions in 4 different situations. You will be randomly paired with another anonymous participant. Your decisions in one decision situation have no influence on other situations.

In each decision situation, you and your paired participant need to make decisions SEQUENTIALLY. That is, one of you and your paired participant is the First Mover who needs to make decision first, and the another is the Second Mover who will make decision after observing the First Mover's choice. Thus, the Points of you and your paired participant will be jointly determined by both of your decisions!

Before asking you to make decisions, we first allocate your role (whether you are the First Mover or the Second Mover).

At the end of the experiment, we will RANDOMLY choose one decision situation’s final Points (determined by your and your randomly paired participant’s choices) as your additional participant fee.

Once a decision situation ends, you can move to another new decision situation.
