Structure of the block.

  • In this block there are 2 active players ROW and COLUMN.
  • You will participate in 5 decision problems.
  • Each decision problem is equally likely to be selected for payment.
  • Thus, you will face 10 rounds:
    • assuming role of the ROW player, and
    • assuming role of the COLUMN player.

Structure of the round:

  • There are two players in each game in this block.
  • ROW player:
    • moves first
    • chooses A or B
  • COLUMN player:
    • observes the choice of ROW player, then
    • decides whether to choose A or B
  • The table below provides an example of the payoff structure depending on the decisions of ROW and COLUMN players
Column choice (The other)
A B
Your payment Column payment Your payment Column payment
Your choice (Row) A 35 tokens 40 tokens 20 tokens 50 tokens
B 60 tokens 20 tokens 30 tokens 25 tokens

  • That is, if ROW player chooses:
    • A, then if COLUMN player chooses:
      • A, then ROW player would receive 35 tokens and COLUMN player would receive 40 tokens
      • B, then ROW player would receive 20 tokens and COLUMN player would receive 50 tokens
    • B, then if COLUMN player chooses:
      • A, then ROW player would receive 60 tokens and COLUMN player would receive 20 tokens
      • B, then ROW player would receive 30 tokens and COLUMN player would receive 25 tokens

Decision of the ROW player:

  • As the ROW player you would see the following interface:
    • Column choice (The other)
          A       B
    Your payment Column payment Your payment Column payment
    Your choice (Row) A 35 tokens 40 tokens 20 tokens 50 tokens
    B 60 tokens 20 tokens 30 tokens 25 tokens
  • Once you take the decision you can proceed to the next decision problem.

    • Decision of the COLUMN player:

  • As the COLUMN player you need to take decision of A or B
  • Given that the decision can be conditioned on the action of the ROW player, you would be presented with the following interface:
    • Your choice (Column)
          A       B
    Column payment Your payment Column payment Your payment
    Row's choice       A 35 tokens 40 tokens 20 tokens 50 tokens
          B 60 tokens 20 tokens 30 tokens 25 tokens
    Your choice (Column)
          A       B
    Column payment Your payment Column payment Your payment
    Row's choice       A 35 tokens 40 tokens 20 tokens 50 tokens
          B 60 tokens 20 tokens 30 tokens 25 tokens
    • That is, you need to take two decisions:
      • Choose A or B if ROW player chose A (the above table)
      • Choose A or B if ROW player chose B (the below table)
    • Note that both decisions are important to determine the outcome as until the end of the experiment you do not know what the ROW player would choose.

    Payments:

    • The payments for each round are determined as follows.
    • You are randomly matched with another player in the room.
    • You are randomly assigned a role (ROW or COLUM).
    • The decision you and another player made would be implemented (in the corresponding roles) and thus the payoff would be determined.
    • One of the rounds will be selected for payment.
    • You will only see outcomes in the end of the experiment.
