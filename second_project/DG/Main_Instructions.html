Welcome to ESSEXLab

  • Welcome and thank you for participating in this experiment!
  • Please read the Instructions carefully. They are identical for all participants in this experiment.
  • Please switch off your mobile phone and place all of your personal belongings away.
  • Please do not attempt to browse or refresh the web or use programs unrelated to the experiment, use the computer only as instructed.
  • Please do not socialize or talk during the experiment. Should you have any questions, please, raise your hand. Our researchers will come to you and answer your questions.

Guidelines

  • You have each earned a £5 payment for showing up on time.
  • You can earn more. How much you can earn in this experiment depends on your decisions, the decisions of other participants and random chance.
  • All your decisions will be treated confidentially. You will be paid in private and in cash at the end of the experiment.
  • This experiment consists of Three Blocks. Each block will be precluded by a set of instructions as well as a comprehension check.

How final earnings are computed

  • You will receive £5 for showing up on time (as the show-up fee).
  • At the end of the experiment one decision-making problem from each block will be randomly selected for payment.
  • All decision-making problems in each block are equally likely to be selected.
  • You will be paid privately and in cash at the end of the experiment.

Further information

  • At the bottom of each page, you may see a Next button after a period of time.
  • You can only move to the next page when a Next button appears.
  • You can take as much time as you need to read and consider the content. There is no time limit on how long you can stay on the page.
  • Enjoy the experiment!
